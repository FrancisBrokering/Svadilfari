Svadilfari is a Safari extension that lets you control Safari with Gestures.

By drawing gestures on websites, you can perform various actions in Safari, such as manipulating tabs, reloading, and copying URLs.

With Svadilfari installed, you can do the following
- Draw configured gestures on websites and perform actions
- Choose from a wide variety of patterns to use
- Register your favorite patterns by drawing them with your finger
- Perform over 10 customizable actions
- Use exclusion lists to disable gestures on specific domains and web pages.

Install Svadilfari, and enjoy a smooth web browsing experience!
